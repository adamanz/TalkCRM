# TalkCRM Product Roadmap

## Vision

**"The Complete SDR Cockpit"** - AI that dials, coaches, and updates your CRM automatically.

Transform from a voice CRM tool to a full sales productivity platform that combines the power of parallel dialing (like Salesfinity) with unique voice-first CRM entry.

## Phase 2: Dialing
**Target: Weeks 5-8**

Add power dialing capabilities to compete with Salesfinity, Orum, and Nooks.

### Core Features
- [ ] **Power Dialer** - Single-line auto-dial through contact lists
- [ ] **Click-to-Dial** - One-click dialing from Salesforce records
- [ ] **Voicemail Detection** - AI-powered detection to skip voicemails
- [ ] **Call Outcome Tracking** - Automatic disposition logging
- [ ] **Local Presence** - Match area code to prospect's location

### Integrations
- [ ] Apollo.io list import
- [ ] ZoomInfo integration
- [ ] Salesforce list views sync
- [ ] Outreach/Salesloft sequences support

## Phase 3: Intelligence
**Target: Weeks 9-12**

- [ ] **AI Call Transcription** - Real-time speech-to-text

## Success Metrics

### North Star
**Qualified Meetings Booked per SDR per Week**

### Key Metrics
1. **Activation**: % of signups who make 10+ calls
2. **Engagement**: Calls per user per day
3. **Retention**: 30-day, 90-day retention rates
4. **Revenue**: MRR, ARPU, Net Revenue Retention
5. **Efficiency**: Connect rate improvement, time saved per rep

## Go-To-Market Strategy

### Distribution Channels
1. **Founder-led LinkedIn** - Daily content about cold calling and SDR productivity
2. **Salesforce AppExchange** - Primary distribution channel
3. **SEO/Content** - Target "parallel dialer for salesforce", "voice CRM", etc.
4. **Integrations** - Outreach, Salesloft, Apollo, ZoomInfo marketplaces
5. **Community** - SDR Slack/Discord, Cold Call Clinic webinars

### Target Customer
- **Company**: B2B SaaS, 10-200 employees
- **Buyer**: VP Sales, Sales Manager, RevOps
- **User**: SDRs, BDRs, Inside Sales Reps
- **Stack**: Salesforce + Outreach/Salesloft + Apollo/ZoomInfo
