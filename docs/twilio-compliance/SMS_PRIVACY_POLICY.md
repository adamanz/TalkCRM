## 1. Introduction

This SMS Privacy Policy explains how TalkCRM ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information related to our SMS verification service. This policy supplements our general Privacy Policy and specifically addresses our SMS messaging practices.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Phone Number
When you sign up for TalkCRM or add a phone number to your account, we collect the phone number you provide for verification purposes.

### 2.2 Verification Data
We collect and temporarily store:
- Verification codes generated for your phone number
- Timestamp of verification requests
- Number of verification attempts
- Verification status (pending, completed, failed)

### 2.3 SMS Delivery Data
We may receive delivery status information from our SMS provider (Twilio) including:
- Message delivery confirmation
- Delivery failures or errors
- Carrier information

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your phone number and related SMS data exclusively for:

| Purpose | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Identity Verification** | Confirming you own the phone number provided |
| **Account Security** | Enabling phone-based authentication for our voice services |
| **Service Delivery** | Allowing you to access TalkCRM via phone call |
| **Fraud Prevention** | Preventing unauthorized account creation |
| **Customer Support** | Responding to your opt-out or help requests |

**We DO NOT use your phone number for:**
- Marketing or promotional messages
- Selling to third parties
- Sharing with advertisers
- Any purpose unrelated to verification and authentication

---

## 4. Information Sharing

### 4.1 Service Providers
We share your phone number with Twilio, Inc., our SMS service provider, solely for the purpose of delivering verification messages. Twilio is contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for message delivery.

### 4.2 No Sale of Data
**We do not sell, rent, or trade your phone number or SMS data to any third party for marketing or any other purpose.**

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our legal rights.

---

## 5. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Phone Number | Retained while your account is active; deleted upon account deletion |
| Verification Codes | Automatically deleted after 10 minutes or upon successful verification |
| Verification Attempts | Retained for 24 hours for rate limiting, then deleted |
| Delivery Logs | Retained for 30 days for troubleshooting, then deleted |

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your phone number and SMS data:

- **Encryption:** Phone numbers are encrypted at rest and in transit
- **Access Controls:** Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis
- **Secure Infrastructure:** Data stored on SOC 2 compliant infrastructure
- **Code Expiration:** Verification codes automatically expire after 10 minutes
- **Attempt Limits:** Maximum 3 verification attempts per code to prevent brute force attacks

---

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Opt-Out
You can stop receiving SMS messages at any time:
- Reply **STOP** to any message
- Remove your phone number in account settings
- Email support@talkcrm.ai

### 7.2 Access and Deletion
You may request:
- Access to the phone numbers associated with your account
- Deletion of your phone number from our systems
- Information about how your data has been used

To make a request, email privacy@talkcrm.ai.

### 7.3 Data Portability
Upon request, we can provide your phone number data in a portable format.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

TalkCRM is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect phone numbers from children. If we discover we have collected information from a child, we will delete it promptly.
