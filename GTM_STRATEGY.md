# TalkCRM Go-To-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

TalkCRM is a voice-first AI assistant for Salesforce that enables sales reps to manage their CRM hands-free. This GTM strategy outlines how to position, price, and sell TalkCRM to achieve $150K MRR within 12 months.

---

## The Problem We Solve

### The CRM Tax
- Sales reps spend **5.5 hours per week** on CRM data entry
- **70% of a sales rep's time** is spent on non-selling activities
- CRM updates get delayed because reps are away from their desks
- Results: stale pipeline data, frustrated reps, inaccurate forecasts

### The Pain by Persona

| Persona | Pain Point |
|---------|------------|
| **Sales Rep** | "I hate updating Salesforce. I do it at the end of the week and forget half of what happened." |
| **Sales Manager** | "My pipeline is never accurate. Reps update deals days after conversations happen." |
| **VP Sales** | "We're paying $150/seat for Salesforce but only 40% of fields are filled out." |
| **RevOps** | "Data quality is killing our forecasting. We can't trust the numbers." |

## Target Market

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Company Characteristics:**
- B2B SaaS or professional services
- 10-200 employees (SMB/Mid-Market)
- Already using Salesforce (any edition)
- Sales team of 5-50 reps
- Field sales or high-velocity inside sales motion

**Tech Stack Signals:**
- Salesforce CRM
- Outreach, Salesloft, or Apollo
- ZoomInfo or similar data enrichment
- Gong, Chorus, or similar call recording

**Behavioral Triggers:**
- Recently hired new sales reps
- Implementing sales methodology (MEDDIC, BANT)
- Complained about CRM adoption on LinkedIn/G2
- Searching for "Salesforce automation" or "sales productivity"

### Buyer Personas

#### Primary Buyer: VP of Sales / Sales Director
- **Goal:** Hit quota, improve rep productivity
- **Metric they own:** Revenue, pipeline coverage, win rate
- **Why they buy:** "Get my reps 5 hours/week back for selling"

#### Economic Buyer: CFO / CEO (at smaller companies)
- **Goal:** Maximize sales ROI
- **Metric they own:** CAC, revenue per rep
- **Why they buy:** "Better ROI on our Salesforce investment"

#### Champion: Sales Manager / RevOps
- **Goal:** Clean data, accurate forecasts
- **Metric they own:** Data hygiene scores, forecast accuracy
- **Why they buy:** "Finally get reps to actually update the CRM"

#### End User: Sales Rep (SDR/AE)
- **Goal:** Hit quota with less admin work
- **Metric they own:** Calls made, meetings booked, deals closed
- **Why they buy:** "Update Salesforce from my car in 30 seconds"

## Competitive Positioning

### Landscape

| Category | Competitors | TalkCRM Differentiation |
|----------|-------------|------------------------|
| **Voice Assistants** | Einstein Voice (discontinued), generic voice-to-text | Purpose-built for sales workflows, not generic dictation |
| **Sales Dialers** | Salesfinity, Orum, Nooks | Voice CRM + dialing (not just parallel dial) |
| **CRM Automation** | Dooly, Scratchpad, Rattle | Voice-first (no typing), works from anywhere |
| **Conversation Intelligence** | Gong, Chorus, Clari | We update CRM; they just record/analyze |

### Competitive Moat
1. **Voice-first UX** — No one else does hands-free CRM at this level
2. **Real-time Salesforce sync** — Not a separate database
3. **AI-powered intent** — Claude understands natural language queries
4. **Mobile-native** — Works from any phone, no app required

### Positioning Statement
> For sales teams who use Salesforce, TalkCRM is the voice AI assistant that lets reps update their CRM hands-free. Unlike manual data entry or note-taking apps, TalkCRM works by voice from anywhere—saving 5+ hours per week per rep while keeping pipeline data accurate in real-time.

## Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Target | Features |
|------|-------|--------|----------|
| **Starter** | Free | Individual reps testing | 50 voice commands/month, 1 user |
| **Pro** | $99/seat/mo | Small teams (5-10) | Unlimited voice, power dialer, transcription |
| **Team** | $199/seat/mo | Growing teams (10-30) | + Live coaching, analytics, manager dashboard |
| **Enterprise** | $299/seat/mo | Large orgs (30+) | + SSO, API, dedicated support, custom fields |

### Pricing Rationale
- **Anchored to value:** $99/seat saves ~$2,700/month in rep time
- **Competitive:** Less than Gong ($100-150), more than Dooly ($25-35) — justified by voice + dialer
- **Land and expand:** Free tier creates pipeline; Pro converts individuals; Team/Enterprise captures orgs

## Go-To-Market Motions

### Phase 1: Founder-Led Sales (Months 1-6)

**Goal:** 50 paying customers, $25K MRR

**Channels:**
1. **LinkedIn Outbound** (Primary)
   - Target: Sales leaders at Salesforce-using companies
   - Content: Daily posts on sales productivity, CRM pain
   - DMs: Personalized outreach with video demos

2. **Warm Intros**
   - Leverage investor/advisor networks
   - Ask early customers for referrals

3. **Product Hunt Launch**
   - Coordinate with early users for upvotes
   - Target "Product of the Day"

**Sales Process:**
- Discovery call (15 min) → Demo (30 min) → Trial (14 days) → Close
- No enterprise motion yet—fast, self-serve trials

### Phase 2: Repeatable Sales (Months 6-12)

**Goal:** 300 customers, $150K MRR

**Channels:**
1. **Salesforce AppExchange**
   - Get listed and reviewed
   - Target "Salesforce productivity" searches

2. **Content Marketing**
   - SEO: "Salesforce voice assistant," "CRM automation"
   - Blog: Sales productivity tips, customer stories
   - YouTube: Demo videos, use case walkthroughs

3. **Partnerships**
   - Salesforce consultants/SIs
   - Sales training companies (Sandler, Challenger)
   - Complementary tools (Outreach, Apollo)

4. **Paid Acquisition**
   - LinkedIn Ads to Sales Leaders
   - Retargeting website visitors
   - G2/Capterra presence

**Sales Team:**
- Hire 1-2 AEs at $100K+ MRR
- Focus on inbound + warm outbound

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 12-24)

**Goal:** 1,000 customers, $500K MRR

**Motions:**
- Outbound SDR team
- Channel partnerships
- Enterprise sales motion
- Customer success for expansion
